Keyi Zhang

I am a recent PhD graduate in Computer Science from Stanford University, where I was fortunate enough to be advised by Mark Horowitz. During my PhD I worked on various software systems for accelerators and my dissertation was on source-level debugging for hardware generator frameworks. Personal interests include reverse-engineering random tech stuff, tinkering, and comic books.
